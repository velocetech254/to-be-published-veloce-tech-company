Why Choose Miti Masters?

Locally Sourced Kenyan Timber

We use only sustainably harvested wood from certified Kenyan forests – Elgon Teak, Meru Oak, Mugavu, and Cypress. No imported particle board, no cheap veneers. Our furniture is solid wood, through and through.

Master Craftsmanship

Our artisans have an average of 15 years of experience. Every joint is mortise‑and‑tenon, every surface hand‑sanded, every finish applied by hand. The result is furniture that lasts decades, not years.

Direct Workshop Prices

By selling directly from our workshop in Industrial Area, Nairobi, we cut out middlemen and retail markups. You get premium quality at prices comparable to mass‑produced factory furniture.

5‑Year Structural Warranty

We stand behind our work. Every piece comes with a 5‑year warranty against structural defects. If something goes wrong, we repair or replace it – no questions asked.

Fully Customisable

Unlike imported furniture that comes in fixed sizes and finishes, we can build to your exact specifications. Adjust dimensions, wood type, stain colour, hardware – we tailor everything to your home.

Free Nairobi Delivery & Installation

We deliver and assemble your furniture in any location within Nairobi free of charge. Our team will place the piece exactly where you want it and remove all packaging.

Compare us to imported furniture: Most furniture sold in Kenyan stores is made from compressed particle board that swells in humidity and breaks within 2‑3 years. Our solid wood pieces are built to withstand Nairobi's climate and last for generations. Plus, you support local jobs and sustainable forestry.
