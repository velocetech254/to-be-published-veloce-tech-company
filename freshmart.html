About FreshMart Supermarket

Founded in 2017 as a small family‑run grocery shop in Umoja Estate, FreshMart has grown into a trusted supermarket chain serving thousands of households across Nairobi. Our mission is simple: make quality groceries accessible and affordable to every Kenyan family.

We partner directly with over 50 local farmers and food producers to bring you the freshest produce at fair prices. Our meat and dairy products are sourced from certified Kenyan ranches, while our grains, flours, and cereals come from reputable millers. We also champion Kenyan brands, stocking a wide range of products from local manufacturers — from tea and coffee to snacks and cleaning supplies.

Beyond retail, FreshMart is committed to giving back. Our "Meals for Schools" initiative has provided over 10,000 meals to children in under‑resourced schools in Nairobi's Eastlands. We also run regular food drives and support community events. When you shop with us, you're helping to build a stronger, healthier Kenya.
