Our Medical Services

General Medicine

Comprehensive health checks, diagnosis, and treatment for adults and children. Walk‑in and scheduled consultations.

Maternity & Antenatal

Complete pregnancy care from first trimester to delivery, including postnatal support, ultrasound, and family planning.

Orthopaedics

Fracture management, joint replacements, sports injuries, and physiotherapy. Our orthopaedic team uses modern minimally invasive techniques.

Cardiology

Heart health check‑ups, ECG, echocardiography, and hypertension management. Early detection and lifestyle counselling.

Dental Care

Oral examinations, cleaning, fillings, extractions, and cosmetic dentistry. Gentle care for adults and children.

Laboratory

Full pathology services including blood tests, urinalysis, malaria screening, and COVID‑19 testing. Fast and reliable results.

Imaging & Radiology

Digital X‑ray, ultrasound, and CT scans on‑site. Reports delivered promptly to your referring doctor.

Emergency Unit

24‑hour casualty department with trained trauma team and ambulance services. We respond to accidents and medical emergencies rapidly.

Why Patients Trust Afya First

Experienced Doctors: Our consultants are Kenyan‑trained with international experience, holding registrations from the Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentists Council.
Minimal Waiting Time: We value your time. Most patients are seen within 20 minutes of their appointment time, and results are shared via SMS.
NHIF & Insurance: We accept NHIF and most private insurance plans including AAR, Jubilee, and Britam. Direct billing available for many covers.
Convenient Location: Situated just off Mombasa Road, with ample parking and wheelchair access. Easy to reach from both the CBD and suburbs.
Patient‑Centred Care: We explain every diagnosis and treatment in plain language. Your comfort and understanding are as important as the medicine.
Modern Equipment: Digital imaging, automated lab analysers, and modern operating theatres mean faster, more accurate results and safer procedures.
