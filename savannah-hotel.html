Our Story

Nestled in the leafy suburb of Karen, just minutes from the Nairobi National Park, Savannah Pride Hotel was born from a passion to blend the raw spirit of the African savannah with the refinement of contemporary luxury. Since opening our doors in 2018, we have welcomed travellers from every corner of the globe, offering them a sanctuary of calm, comfort, and understated elegance.

Our design philosophy draws inspiration from the natural hues of the Kenyan landscape — warm earth tones, rich ochres, and touches of gold that echo the setting sun over the Ngong Hills. Every piece of furniture, every artwork, and every texture has been carefully curated by local artisans to create a sense of place that is authentically Kenyan, yet internationally appealing.

At Savannah Pride, we believe that hospitality is more than just a stay; it is an immersive journey. Whether you are here for a wildlife adventure, a corporate retreat, or a romantic escape, our dedicated team ensures that your every need is met with genuine warmth and meticulous attention to detail. We are committed to sustainable practices, supporting local communities, and preserving the natural environment that makes our location so extraordinary.

Dining at Savannah Pride

From casual poolside bites to multi‑course gourmet dinners, our three distinct restaurants celebrate the richness of Kenyan produce and global culinary traditions.

🍽️ Acacia Grill

Our signature fine‑dining restaurant offers a sophisticated menu that fuses classic European techniques with bold African flavours. Start with the slow‑braised ox‑tail samosa or the Swahili coconut prawn soup, then move on to a perfectly grilled fillet of Lake Victoria tilapia accompanied by sukuma‑wiki pesto. The Acacia Grill boasts an award‑winning wine list featuring South African estates and emerging Kenyan vineyards.

Dinner: 18:30 – 22:30 | Closed Mondays

☕ Terrace Café

The heart of the hotel, our all‑day Terrace Café overlooks the pool and tropical gardens. Here you can enjoy a leisurely breakfast of made‑to‑order eggs benedict with house‑cured salmon, freshly baked pastries, and single‑origin Kenyan coffee. Lunch offers a vibrant selection of salads, wood‑fired pizzas, and the signature Savannah burger. In the afternoon, the café transforms into a serene spot for high tea with scones and clotted cream.

Open daily: 06:30 – 23:00

🍹 Sunset Bar

As the golden hour paints the sky, the Sunset Bar comes alive with the sound of gentle Afro‑jazz and the clink of handcrafted cocktails. Our mixologists infuse local ingredients like baobab, tamarind, and dawa herbs into classic concoctions. Pair your drink with a selection of tapas — from peri‑peri chicken skewers to zesty mango ceviche — as you watch the sun dip below the Ngong Hills. Live acoustic music every Friday and Saturday evening.

Open daily: 16:00 – 00:00

Wellness & Leisure

🏊

Infinity Pool

Our heated infinity pool stretches 25 metres and is surrounded by plush sun loungers and private cabanas. Poolside service from the Sunset Bar ensures you never have to leave your slice of paradise.

🧖

Amani Spa

Inspired by ancient African healing rituals, Amani Spa offers a range of treatments using natural ingredients like marula oil, Rooibos, and Kenyan honey. Choose from massages, facials, and body wraps in one of our four treatment rooms or in the open‑air pavilion.

🏋️

Fitness Centre

Stay active in our state‑of‑the‑art gym equipped with Technogym machines, free weights, and a dedicated yoga and Pilates studio. Personal trainers are available on request, and we offer complimentary yoga sessions every morning at sunrise.

🦁

Safari Experiences

As Nairobi’s only hotel bordering a national park, we offer exclusive game drives with our resident naturalist. Spot lions, rhinos, and giraffes just ten minutes from your room. Half‑day and full‑day excursions can be arranged at the concierge desk.
